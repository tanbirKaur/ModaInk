Shipping Policy

We Deliver products within India. The Delivery is done by our Third Party Delivery Partner. Any items notified by our courier companies to be restricted and/or banned and/or prohibited from time to time.

We aim to dispatch all orders by 72 hours of placing the order, but delays due to payments / courier companies / sale traffic will come under exceptions where the order could take upto 96 hours to be dispatched.

Items once dispatched, cannot be redirected to a different Address or be cancelled. Any action needed will be taken after the Delivery of the item to the Address first entered.

All orders will be Delivered within 15 days of Dispatch.

The custom orders and made to order products may take longer time to dispatch. 

Product prices displayed are inclusive of all taxes.

For further information, please write to us at customersupport@modaink.com
